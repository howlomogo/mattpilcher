ARKO was founded in 1996.

Over the last 20 years, we have developed a large portfolio of beautiful and exciting projects. Our projects range from hotels to hospitals, and from housing schemes to offices.

We have always taken an eco friendly stance on architecture and we always look into taking advantage of new cutting edge technologies that are available.
